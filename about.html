About Louise

Bounceback Physiotherapy is a private physiotherapy practice which offers assessment and treatment of joint and muscle pain, sports injuries, soft tissue massage and rehabilitation.

The business was set up by Louise after returning home from Aberdeen (Scotland). There, Louise worked in a private practice which included working between Elgin and Buckie. She was very excited to move home and start a business in her home town of Castlebar.

Louise began her training with a two year degree in IT Carlow in Physiology and Health Science. After that, she travelled to Aberdeen where she undertook a 4 year degree in Robert Gordon's University in Physiotherapy.
