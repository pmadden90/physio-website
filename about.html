My Story

Louise began her training with a two year Higher Certificate in IT Carlow in Physiology and Health Science. After that, she travelled to Aberdeen where she undertook a 4 year degree in Robert Gordon University in Physiotherapy.

Louise began her professional career working in a private clinic in the north east of Scotland, where she had the fortune of working with a great team, who helped her develop into a confident and competent Physiotherapist.

Louise has experience dealing with sports injuries, repetitive strain issues, job related problems, pregnancy related pain, back pain, arthritis and many other conditions.
